## Outcome

Gemma4 E4B QAT Q4 fits into 6 GB VRAM at `n_ctx=130048`, but it is not currently
a good Hermes-agent default on this machine. Even a two-token short smoke
request is slow, and the Hermes-style routing prompt timed out after 420
seconds.

Gemma4 E4B QAT Q2 is meaningfully better than Q4 for warm and Hermes-routing
requests, completing the routing prompt instead of timing out. It is still much
slower than the Qwen3.5 2B Q4 XL baseline, which completed the same
Hermes-routing preset in about 6 seconds after warmup.

Keep Q2 and Q4 as experimental quality candidates. For active local-agent work,
continue to prefer the already measured 2B-class profiles until E4B QAT is
retested under lower load, smaller context, or newer llama.cpp/runtime settings.
