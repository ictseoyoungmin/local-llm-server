# 2026-06-06 Gemma4 E4B QAT Download and Benchmark

## Goal

Evaluate the newly published Gemma 4 E4B QAT GGUF as a possible higher-quality
local-agent model for 6 GB VRAM. The user observed that 4B Q4 models caused
system stalls while other containers/processes were active, so non-QAT 4B
benchmarks are deferred until the machine is otherwise idle.

Source checked:

- https://huggingface.co/unsloth/gemma-4-E4B-it-qat-GGUF/tree/main

## Outcome

Gemma4 E4B QAT fits into 6 GB VRAM at `n_ctx=130048`, but it is not currently a
good Hermes-agent default on this machine. Even a two-token short smoke request
is slow, and the Hermes-style routing prompt timed out after 420 seconds.

Keep this profile as an experimental quality candidate. For active local-agent
work, continue to prefer the already measured 2B-class profiles until E4B QAT
is retested under lower load, smaller context, or newer llama.cpp/runtime
settings.
